“The home of beautiful and functional modular kitchens”

Our kitchens are designed with the aim of making your daily cooking experience easier and enjoyable. At Terazzo, we understand that every home has unique kitchen needs, which is why we offer a range of modular kitchen types and layouts to suit your style, space, and budget.

Types of Modular Kitchens

Straight Modular Kitchen
Ideal for small kitchens, straight modular kitchens have a simple layout that is easy to navigate. This type of kitchen features a single counter space with cabinets and drawers underneath. It is perfect for small families or individuals who enjoy cooking.
L-Shaped Modular Kitchen
L-shaped modular kitchens are perfect for medium to large spaces. They feature two adjacent counters that form an L-shape, providing ample storage and workspaces. This type of kitchen is ideal for families that enjoy cooking together.
U-Shaped Modular Kitchen
U-shaped modular kitchens are designed for larger spaces and provide maximum storage and workspace. This type of kitchen features three walls of cabinets and counters, forming a U-shape. It is perfect for large families or individuals who love cooking and entertaining guests.
Parallel Modular Kitchen
Parallel modular kitchens are perfect for long and narrow spaces. They feature two parallel counters with a walkway in between, providing an efficient workspace. This type of kitchen is ideal for individuals who prefer to cook alone or small families.
