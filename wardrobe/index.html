Wardrobe

Get all of your closet needs met at Terazzo's Design Ideas!

Our ergonomic, modular closets are available in a variety of colors, finishes, and materials. Our closet plans prioritize efficiency and user friendliness while still promising to be visually striking additions to your bedroom.
It’s time to start thinking about a stylish, original, and up-to-date layout for your wardrobe, one that will help you to neatly organize all of your garments and accessories. If you’re looking to spice up your home’s decor, look no further than Terazzo Design Concepts’ extensive selection of contemporary modular wardrobes.
Let's take a closer look at modular wardrobes and the many ways they may spice up your bedroom!
At Terazzo, we offer a wide range of wardrobe types that cater to various design preferences, storage needs, and space constraints. Our most popular types of wardrobes include:
Sliding Modular Wardrobe
Our sliding modular wardrobe is perfect for those who want a contemporary look and a space-saving solution. With sliding doors, it is easy to access your clothes and accessories while keeping the space around the wardrobe free.
Hinged Modular Wardrobe
For those who prefer traditional hinged doors, our hinged modular wardrobe is the perfect choice. The doors can be customized to your desired style and color, ensuring that your wardrobe perfectly complements your bedroom decor.
Modular Walk-In Wardrobe
Our modular walk-in wardrobe is a dream come true for those who need extra storage space. With customizable shelves, drawers, and hanging space, you can create a wardrobe that fits your specific needs and preferences.
Mirrored Modular Wardrobe
Our mirrored modular wardrobe not only offers ample storage but also adds the illusion of more space to your bedroom. The mirrored doors reflect light, making your bedroom appear larger and brighter.
Wooden Wardrobe
Our wooden wardrobe is a classic and timeless option that adds warmth and texture to your bedroom. Choose from a range of wood finishes, including oak, mahogany, and walnut, to create a wardrobe that suits your style.
White Wardrobe
If you are looking for a wardrobe that adds a touch of elegance and sophistication to your room, our white wardrobe is just what you need. With its clean lines and minimalist design, it exudes a sense of calm and serenity.
Almirah Wardrobe
Our almirah wardrobes are a great choice if you are looking for a wardrobe that is sturdy, durable, and spacious. With multiple compartments and shelves, you can organize your clothes and accessories in a neat and orderly manner.
Freestanding Wardrobe
Our freestanding wardrobes are perfect for those who are constantly on the move. They are easy to assemble and disassemble, making them ideal for people who change houses frequently.
L-shaped Wardrobe
Our L-shaped wardrobes are designed to make the most of your space. With their unique shape, they provide ample storage space while fitting snugly in the corner of your room.
Steel Wardrobe
Our Steel Wardrobe is designed to provide maximum storage space while ensuring durability and strength. The wardrobe is made of high-quality steel and is available in various sizes and colors to suit your needs and preferences.
Hanging Wardrobe
Our Hanging Wardrobe is perfect for those who love to keep their clothes organized and wrinkle-free. The wardrobe comes with a hanging rod and multiple shelves to store your clothes and accessories, ensuring a clutter-free bedroom.
Foldable Wardrobe
Our Foldable Wardrobe is ideal for those who are always on the move or have limited space. The wardrobe is easy to assemble and disassemble, making it perfect for frequent travelers or those living in small apartments.
Wall-mounted Wardrobe
Our Wall-mounted Wardrobe is designed to save space and provide a modern touch to your room. The wardrobe is mounted on the wall and comes with shelves and drawers to store your clothes and accessories.
Modern Room Wardrobes
Our Modern Room Wardrobes are perfect for those who want to add a touch of elegance and sophistication to their bedrooms. The wardrobes come in various designs, colors, and sizes to suit your needs and complement your room’s decor.
Dresser
A dresser is an essential piece of furniture for any bedroom. It provides ample storage space for your clothes, accessories, and other essentials while also adding a touch of sophistication to your room. At Terazzo, we offer a wide range of dressers to choose from, including various sizes, designs, and finishes to suit your personal style.
Wardrobe With Dressing Table
A wardrobe with a dressing table is a perfect combination of functionality and style. It provides ample storage space for your clothes and accessories while also offering a convenient space for you to get ready. Our range of wardrobes with dressing tables comes in various sizes, designs, and finishes to match your taste and decor.
Study Table
A study table is an essential piece of furniture for any home office or study room. It provides a comfortable workspace where you can concentrate on your tasks and be productive. At Terazzo, we offer a variety of study tables that come in different sizes, designs, and finishes to suit your needs and personal style.
Hall Wardrobes
A hall wardrobe is an excellent storage solution for your hallway or entryway. It provides a place to store your coats, shoes, and other essentials while also adding a touch of style to your home. Our range of hall wardrobes comes in various sizes, designs, and finishes to complement your decor and make your hallway more functional.
Kitchen Wardrobes
A kitchen wardrobe is an essential piece of furniture for any kitchen. It provides a place to store your kitchen essentials, such as plates, glasses, and utensils, while also keeping your kitchen organized and clutter-free.
At Terazzo, we offer a range of kitchen wardrobes that come in different sizes, designs, and finishes to match your kitchen decor and make your cooking space more functional.
